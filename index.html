What is uVote?

uVote is an online voting application, that allows registered voters to create, explore, and vote for candidates in upcoming elections. From small polls, to presidential. Join the dozens of other users today!

Start Voting Today!

Why uVote?

uVote is going to change the way elections are held. One of the largest problems it tackles is the declining voter turnout rate, where only 62.3% of registered voters participated in the 2008 election, and 57.5% in 2012.

Statistics provided by The Bipartisan Policy Center

uVote provides everyone with a simple and convient way to vote without ever having to leave the comfort of their home. Our mission is to aliviate the burder of voting using modern technology and design. No more driving to poll locations and standing in line!

Content and Features

Besides just being a place to vote, uVote provides users with an easily accesible place for non-partison information about upcoming elections. Our goal is to keep all our users informed so that they can make the best decision.

Our site is desgined to be used by everyone, from IT professionals, to users who have never used a computer before. Using state of the art design techniques, we make the experience as plesant as possible.
