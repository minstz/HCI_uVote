What is uVote?

uVote is an online voting application, that allows registered voters to create, explore, and vote for candidates in upcoming elections. From small polls, to presidential. Join the dozens of other users today!

Start Voting Today!

Why uVote?

uVote is going to change the way elections are held. One of the largest problems it tackles is the declining voter turnout rate, where only 62.3% of registered voters participated in the 2008 election, and 57.5% in 2012.

Statistics provided by The Bipartisan Policy Center
